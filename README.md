## Featured Projects

### 1. Inventory Optimization via Spectral Graph Theory
*Application of the Laplacian Matrix and Fiedler Vector to minimize search time in complex inventories.*
- **The Problem:** How to organize an auto parts inventory considering withdrawal frequency and purchase affinity between parts?
- **The Solution:** Modeled as a Quadratic Assignment Problem (QAP), spectral relaxation, and use of the Fiedler vector to find the optimal arrangement.

### 3. Macroeconomic Site Selection Index (Car Repair Expansion)
*Multi-criteria decision analysis (MCDA) and economic modeling for geographic market evaluation.*

- **The Problem:** Selecting the optimal geographical province for business expansion when market scale indicators (vehicle fleet, population density) and operational cost barriers (rental prices, CPI, unemployment) conflict.
- **The Solution:** Designed a non-linear composite economic index (S) that balances market attraction against operational drag. The model applies a square root to account for the diminishing marginal utility of vehicle density, weights local purchasing power, and integrates a penalty factor for structural unemployment to rank 50 Spanish provinces.
