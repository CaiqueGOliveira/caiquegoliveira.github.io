## Featured Projects

### 1. Inventory Optimization via Spectral Graph Theory
*Application of the Laplacian Matrix and Fiedler Vector to minimize search time in complex inventories.*

- **The Problem:** How to organize an auto parts inventory considering withdrawal frequency and purchase affinity between parts?
- **The Solution:** Modeled as a Quadratic Assignment Problem (QAP), applying spectral relaxation and the Fiedler vector of the combinatorial graph Laplacian.
